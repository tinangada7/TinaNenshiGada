Animal Rescue

OVERVIEW


Animal Rescue is a mobile application developed to improve the lives of animals by helping them connect to an animal rescue group, organization or individual. The app helps create a world where all companion animals find compassionate and permanent homes.

The initial idea  was to connect with the animal rescue group. These groups take unwanted, abandoned, abused, or stray pets and attempt to find suitable homes for them. But we found difficulties in managing it through the traditional system and hence we wanted to create an application which makes the communication easier and smooth.

The Challenge

Animal shelters have many challenges facing them in their quest to unite abandoned animals with good homes. Battling people’s preconceived notions about rescue pets, public ignorance over curbing animal overpopulation, lack of centralized funding for these non-profit shelters, and the staggering number of animals all contribute to a veritable host of problems to surmount.One of the best ways to help shelters combat this issue is to raise public awareness, and spread information about the difficulties faced in providing such a valuable service. With that in mind, here are three of the major problems facing animal shelters today.


A lack of public awareness

Funding is hard to come by

There are too many pets needing space

Sketches

After identifying the problem areas & user expectations we started building the modules. We created the Low-fidelity wireframing. Wireframing consisted of features the application should include and how it will be designed. We identified the users and created a rough idea of how each screen will look like. Wireframing helped us in each phase of the design. We had a blueprint of the application which modified after every interview and feedback from the users. But it cleared the idea and guided us while programming and visual designs.
