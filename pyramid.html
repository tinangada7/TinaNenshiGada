Pyramid

An application which helps you manage anxiety through mobile application & wearable device.

OVERVIEW

Pyramid is your guide to health and happiness. It’s a simple way to let go of stress and get a better night’s rest. With the help of pyramid application, we are guiding you to do meditations and mindfulness techniques that bring calm, wellness and balance to your life by a few mindfulness activities.

The goal of this project was to come up with an application for SUNY Oswego student an application which allows them to reduce their anxiety, connect with people to talk with when anxiety can’t be managed alone, connect with the general physician if required through mobile application and wearable device.

CHALLENGE

Help people find ways to manage their anxiety through a mobile app that can be used by their family member and physician.

OUTCOME

A simple, friendly app design that helps users find approaches based on their preferences while monitoring and tracking their progress.

Problem:

Anxiety disorders are the most common mental illness in the U.S., affecting 40 million adults - 18.1 percent of the population. They're highly treatable, yet only 36.9 percent of those suffering receive treatment. The pyramid is a mobile Android app that aims to help users manage and reduce their anxiety. It allows you to make your safe zone to connect with people when you can’t control the situation and also sends your report immediately to your physician when needed. The pyramid’s greatest value to the market comes via wearable technology that allows users to track their behaviors, moods, physical factors, and other signs that could contribute to anxiety and that correlate with intense feelings of stress or worry. The design of the wearable device is intentionally minimal, with few screens. It is available in Android and iOS.

Market Research and App Flow

To begin this project, I spent a few hours getting up to speed on anxiety-related apps and trends within the mental health space. I also researched wearable devices and apps with wearable sensor integration. I learned that there are 10,000+ mental health apps on the market. The mental health space is incredibly competitive! The apps cover a broad variety of approaches and specializations. To help identify the market segment that most directly aligns with the pyramid’s positioning, I conducted a competitive analysis. This gave me the opportunity to look for ways that might differentiate itself and provide unique value to users.
